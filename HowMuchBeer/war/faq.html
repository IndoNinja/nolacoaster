Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to log in?

No, definitely not. You can always report your beer consumption anonymously. If, however, you decide to log in, your reports will be weighed more highly in the results that others see. This is a very simple means of spam detection, or at least avoidance. We will never email you, sell your info or do anything else unscrupulous with your information.

Who are you?

Just a man who likes beer. But feel free to contact me with your hate mail, fan mail, bug reports, etc.
